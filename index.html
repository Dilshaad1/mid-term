Our Story

Kin Kin Bakery is a humble Chinese Bakery Business that sells Hong Kong style buns, pastries, egg tarts, cakes, slushes, smoothies and bubble tea. The company started off as just a bubble tea shop located at Woodside Square Mall back in 2001. Since then, Kin Kin has expanded its business to include Chinese Bakery. Kin Kin opened up its first bakery on September 2009 at Agincourt Mall in Scarborough. They have many locations around the GTA including Scarborough, North York and Downtown.

What we Supply

At Ki Kin Bakery, we craft heavenly confections that are sure to tantalize your taste buds and warm your heart. With a perfect blend of passion and expertise, our skilled bakers create a delightful array of pastries, bread, cakes, and more to cater to your cravings.
